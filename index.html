About Me

Hello my name is Jennifer and I am a self-taught front-end developer. While I graduated with a degree in Zoology I found my interests better aligned with programming and front-end development work. I love the never ending quest that front-end development provides and am committed to becoming a student for life. Nothing can quite match the feeling when you finally solve a tricky problem or get something to work just right. My experience in Zoology has helped me transition into programming with a customer focused mind. Neither animals or customers can tell you exactly what they need. My skills include JavaScript, HTML, and CSS. In my free time I enjoy reading books, playing Magic the Gathering, and watching esports.
